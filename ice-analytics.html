Goal

Democratize data access so Product Managers can answer "Did metrics improve?" without engineering delays.

What I Built

1. Health Check

High-level DAU and Revenue pulse for daily standups.

2. Economy Monitor

Sinks vs. Sources visualization to detect inflation.

3. Feature Funnels

Conversion tracking for specific user flows.

Key Metrics Tracked

  • ARPDAU (Average Revenue Per Daily Active User): The north star for monetization health.
  • LTV (Lifetime Value): Projected value based on D7/D30 cohorts.
  • Stickiness (DAU/MAU): Measuring habit formation.
  • Conversion Rate: % of users who make a first purchase.

Insight & Result

V1 failed because it had too many charts. I rebuilt V2 to show only Red/Green health indicators, doubling adoption by the design team.
