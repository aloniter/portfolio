ICE Analytics

Internal KPI dashboard for product and content teams at ICE

Internal Tool • Data & Product Analytics • Media Platform

Overview

ICE Analytics is an internal analytics dashboard built to help ICE employees quickly understand product and content performance. The tool centralized key KPIs into a single, self-serve view, reducing reliance on manual reports and ad-hoc data requests.

Role

Product Owner • Data Analyst

I defined KPI requirements with stakeholders, implemented metric logic, and designed the dashboard structure to support daily decision-making for non-technical users.

The Problem

As ICE grew, teams relied on multiple spreadsheets and manual data pulls to understand performance. KPI definitions were inconsistent, insights were slow to surface, and non-technical employees depended heavily on engineers or analysts for answers.

The Solution

I built an internal dashboard that centralized core KPIs into clear, consistent views. The focus was on fast readability, shared metric definitions, and making insights accessible to product, content, and design teams without technical support.

What the Dashboard Includes

  • Daily and weekly KPI health views
  • Trend analysis for traffic, engagement, and monetization
  • Funnel views for key product and content flows
  • Simple visual indicators for performance changes

Impact

The dashboard improved internal visibility into performance and shortened the time needed to answer common business questions. Teams were able to discuss data using the same definitions, which reduced confusion and improved alignment across departments.

What I Learned

Building ICE Analytics taught me how to translate business questions into measurable KPIs, design dashboards for non-technical users, and iterate based on real employee usage rather than assumptions.
